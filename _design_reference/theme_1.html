Welcome to Our Church Family

Where believers gather in faith and unity

We are delighted to welcome you to Grace Community Church, a place dedicated to spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ and fostering spiritual growth. Our congregation believes in the power of faith, the importance of community, and the transformative message of God's love. Whether you are exploring faith for the first time, returning after a long absence, or seeking a deeper connection with your church community, you will find a warm welcome here. We gather together in worship, study the scriptures, support one another through life's challenges, and work toward building a kingdom that reflects God's values of compassion, justice, and unconditional love. Our church is committed to serving not only our congregation but also the broader community through charitable work, outreach programs, and spiritual guidance. We believe that faith in action creates meaningful change in the world. Come as you are, and join us in celebrating our shared journey of faith and the hope we find through Jesus Christ.

Our Story

Founded in faith, growing in grace

Our History

Grace Community Church was founded in 1995 with a simple mission: to create a welcoming space where people could encounter God's love and grow in their faith. What started as a small group of believers meeting in a living room has blossomed into a vibrant community of over 2,000 members.

Throughout our journey, we have remained committed to our core values: biblical teaching, authentic worship, genuine community, and compassionate service to others.
